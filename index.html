Welcome to Neon 80s Park

We have the perfect 80s themed entertainment park for you. If you are longing for 80s music, dancing, fashion and games we've got you covered.
From classic Dungeons and Dragons events to Roller Discos to glow in the dark soccer the family will be entertained. For adults we have an awesome cocktail bar and a dance club.

Our founders believe that the 80s had the best music, movies and fashion. We aim to please our visitors with an unforgetable entertainment experience.

Retro Disco with 80s dance music

We have the best 80s tunes. Open from 8pm till 4am every day of the week, the party never stops at our Retro Disco. If you are lucky you might catch world famous celebrities partying at our Disco.

Occasionally we have a roller disco event, where folk can hire skates and skate around to classic tunes.

Classic Dungeons and Dragons

Come sign up for weekly Dungeons and Dragons sessions and events. Regardless if you prefer playing as a Halfling Barbarian or High Elf Wizard we have the right group for you.

We cater for all players from beginners who are learning the rules to seasoned veterans. No need to bring any material, we even have dice for you to use.
